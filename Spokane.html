Spokane

Spokane is a city located in Spokane county in eastern Washington.
It was incorporated November 29th, 1881. The current population is approximatley 230,160.
Spokane is considered to be an urban city and the second most populous city in Washington.
The average income is Spokane is $62,287 which is lower than the state average.
However, the cost of living is lower so it balances out.
The most important industries in Spokane are as follows...

  1. Aerospace
  2. Agribusiness
  3. Enviromental Technology

The climate of Spokane is much
drier and hot than that
of western Washington state.
