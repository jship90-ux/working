Olympia

Olympia is the Capital of Washington state. It was incoporated on January 28th, 1859. It has a population of 55,605
and is located in Thurston county. Olympia sits at
the southern portion of the Puget Sound. Olympia is most often
described as a mix between urban and suburban. The median household
income in Olympia is lower than the state average
being $67,975 while the state's is $83,655.

Olympia sits at the southern
end of the Puget Sound and has
