Seattle

Seattle is the located in the Puget Sound and is located in King county.
The population is 749,256. It was incorporated on January 14th, 1865.
The median household income in Seattle is $115,000 which is higher than the state average.
Seattle is an urban city. Main attractions in Seattle include but are not limited to...

  • Space Needle
  • Pike Place Market
  • The Museum of Flight

Though Olympia is the capital of Washington state,
Seattle is often considered to
be the cultural capital.
