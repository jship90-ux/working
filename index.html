Washington State

Washington State, also known as the Evergreen state, is located in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States.
It was officially admitted into the union in 1889, making it the 42nd state to do so. It is bordered by Canada to the north,
Idaho to the east, and Oregon to the south.
It earned its title as the evergreen state from its large concentration of Evergreen trees. One famous feature of this state is Mt. Rainier.
